Simplified Mobile-based Rice Crop Manager Application.

The objective is to assess the current technology utilized by rice farmers in Davao Oriental and develop a simplified version for a mobile platform. Farmers can use the program on their Android smartphones to examine their specific location and receive recommendations based on the available information. Additionally, aspiring rice farmers will have the opportunity to learn the correct techniques for growing rice, from planting to harvesting.
